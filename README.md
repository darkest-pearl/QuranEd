# QuranEd

QuranEd is a multilingual Qur'an learning platform focused on making Qur'an education easier to access, more affordable, and available to students across different cultures and languages.

## Mission

Make Qur'an learning accessible to people who cannot easily afford expensive classes, while also supporting qualified teachers and volunteers.

## MVP goals

- Explain the QuranEd mission clearly.
- Collect student interest and teacher/volunteer applications.
- Present a simple teacher matching model by language, learning goal, and affordability.
- Prepare the project for authentication, dashboards, bookings, and payments in later phases.

## Core users

- Students who want to learn reading, tajweed, memorization, or Qur'an correction.
- Teachers and volunteers who want to teach Qur'an in different languages.
- Admins who verify teachers, manage applications, and keep the platform safe.

## Development stack

Initial MVP: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS.

Future backend recommendation: Supabase for authentication, database, teacher profiles, bookings, and payment records.
